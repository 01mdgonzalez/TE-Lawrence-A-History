Lawrence's Military Campaign

This dataset outlines T.E. Lawrence's military endeavors during World War I, delineated by the countries in which he operated, along with their respective start and end years and durations in months. Lawrence's involvement commenced in Egypt from 1914 to 1916, spanning a period of 24 months. Subsequently, his activities extended to Arabia from 1916 to 1918, with an equivalent duration of 24 months. Transitioning to Palestine, Lawrence's presence persisted from 1917 to 1918, encompassing a period of 12 months. Finally, his engagements in Syria unfolded from 1918 to 1919, constituting another 12-month duration. This dataset provides a comprehensive overview of Lawrence's multifaceted military campaigns across various regions throughout the course of World War I.
